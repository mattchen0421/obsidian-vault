- R<sub>2</sub> is a hydrogen (imipenem)
	- substrate for renal dehydropeptidase-1 (DHP-1)
	- hydrolysis
- R2 is a R methyl, 
	- stable to DHP-1

	- 增加對 transpeptidase 和 ß-lactamase 的反應性
- C5 C6 trans 和 C6 hydroxyethyl
	- ß-lactamase 穩定性
- 四個以上不對稱中心
