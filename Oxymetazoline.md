- α<sub>1</sub> stimulation
	- ↑ vasoconstriction
- α<sub>2A</sub> (mostly manifested at high dose)
- Clinical Use:
	- Topical nasal decongestant 減充血劑
	- Epistaxis 流鼻血
- Side effects
	- Rebound congestion 鼻塞(limit use to less than 3days)
	- “Rhinitis medicamentosa” 藥物性鼻炎
	- Headache
	- Insomnia 失眠
