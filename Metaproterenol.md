- A moderately selective β<sub>2</sub>-adrenergic receptor agonist
- Chemically similar to [[isoproterenol]] but not a catecholamine
- Also known as orciprenaline (INN)
- Clinical Use
	- Treatment of asthma 哮喘
- Side Effects
	- Tremor 顫抖症
	- Tachycardia
