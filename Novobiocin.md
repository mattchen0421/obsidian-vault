## SAR
- Aglycon: novobiocic acid
- sugar moiety: noviose
## 特色
- from Streptomyces spheroides and S. niveus
- bacteriostatic

- 對 G-(+) 和 Proteus vulgaris 活性
- 副作用
	- urticaria 蕁麻疹
	- allergic rashes
	- hepatotoxicity
	- blood dyscrasias 血液惡病質
